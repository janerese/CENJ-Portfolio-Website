Synopsis

“Maze Runner” is an action-packed game that puts the player in the shoes of a brave warrior - either Barbarian or Amazon. The objective of the game is to navigate through a perilous maze to collect the golden keys and escape the maze under time pressure. The player must avoid the powerful snake monsters as they can take one point from their life, but on the brighter side, there are crystals that can add one. The maze also drains their time, making it crucial for the player to move quickly. Some keys are old ones that reduce points, while others increase points for the warrior. Same goes with the timer; some time objects in the field can increase time, while others reduce the allotted time. The player can also find a mysterious potion that provides random attributes that will help them overcome the labyrinth.

The game challenges the player’s strategic thinking, logical thinking, and concentration to be able to escape the maze within the time limit with the amount of points they can obtain. Overall, “Maze Runner” is an intense and thrilling game that will test the player’s determination to finish the quest.

Characters

The game is a one-player game but the player can choose between two different characters: the Amazon and the Barbarian. Each character has different attributes that the player can choose from.

  • Amazon - Weaker Health but Faster Speed
  • Barbarian - Slower Speed but Stronger Health
